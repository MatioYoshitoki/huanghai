MMAP(Information database of Marine Microorganism Active Product)

The Information database of MMAP is an information resource for marine microorganism active products. It gives us the basic experimental and theoretical data for the active products that originate from marine microorganism, including the sub database of marine microorganism, marine enzyme and the resources from deep sea.Please use the Menu in the bottom to the sub database.
